Seth is a software developer living in Washington, DC. He likes trashy movies, loud concerts, and spicy bloody marys. He believes in #FreeSpeech and #FreeBeer.

Seth was born and raised in Ohio and went to Case Western Reserve University for a degree in computer science. He's spent the last 10 years in a variety of development roles, from embedded programming for industrial equipment, to support software for heavy lift helicopters, to analytic systems for electronic medical records.

Seth is currently a senior consultant at Excella Consulting where he specializes in .Net and Javascript. He's a regular contributor at local meetups such as Tech Talk DC and NOVA Code Camp. Seth also teaches Front End Web Development at General Assembly.
